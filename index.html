Winterrain Technologies, Ludhiana, Punjab

Full Stack Software Engineer

Aug 2014-Dec 2014

The project was for a US client, TechBank, a company that purchases old devices and sells them on Ebay. The solution was end to end, from price prediction at the booth based on device condition, to shipping to head office, to finally putting it on Ebay after confirmation that the device isn’t stolen.

Upon arrival of customer, the system would suggest a price based on previous buy/sell trend, device condition and other factors. Once bought, the system would print an invoice for the customer, print a label for shipping to head office and put the IMEI number on LeadsOnline to check for theft.

Once the 3-day freeze period from LeadsOnline ends, the device is put on Ebay for reselling using its API. If the device is too damaged, it would be sold for parts and for full price otherwise. Upon getting sold, the device is on its way. If it is not sold within the prescribed period, the price is lowered and put back on Ebay.

The system also generated audit reports for business optimization and fed back for price prediction.
